# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to the OctoAcme project management documentation! This README provides an overview of our project management processes and links to all documents in the `docs` folder for easy navigation.

## Project Management Processes Summary
OctoAcme adopts a structured yet adaptive approach to project management, blending Agile and Lean principles to achieve efficiency and collaboration. The process begins with **project initiation**, where the scope, goals, stakeholders, and high-level milestones are defined. Early in this phase, roles such as Project Lead, Key Contributors, and Stakeholders are identified, aligning responsibilities and setting expectations. With a strong emphasis on planning, the **project planning phase** breaks down the project into actionable tasks, highlighting dependencies, identifying potential risks, and establishing a clear roadmap for execution. Regular planning sessions ensure that the team remains aligned with client priorities and strategic objectives.

Effective communication and tracking are central to the **execution phase**, where OctoAcme fosters collaboration through periodic status updates and emphasizes flexibility to adapt to changing requirements. The documentation outlines well-defined communication pathways, empowering teams to escalate risks promptly and resolve issues collaboratively. Personas such as Quality Assurance Specialists and Communication Leads play critical roles in maintaining transparency.

Quality assurance is pivotal during the **release and deployment phases**, involving detailed checklists, peer reviews, and automated tests to ensure reliability. Retrospective meetings post-project serve as a critical step, gathering feedback, and committing to continuous improvement.
